About Me

My name is Ari-Matti Toivonen, and I hail from the forests of Finland.
I am a freelance illustrator, concept artist and 2D game artist. I've worked with a broad variety of clients ranging from large companies such as The Teaching Company to small indie operations like SixtyGig Games.

I am deeply passionate about gaming and storytelling, and have been expressing that passion by making art as my full-time profession for nearly 10 years. I like to focus on evoking strong emotions through narrative, and exploring strong themes in my artwork.
My other passion is tabletop roleplaying games, where I put my passion for worldbuilding into practice by being an active Gamemaster. I also self-publish tabletop roleplaying books.

If you're looking for an artist for your project, don't be afraid to get in touch!
